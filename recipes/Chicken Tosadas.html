Chicken Tosadas

Crispy tortillas topped with shredded chicken, beans, lettuce, cheese, and salsa. Quick to assemble and great for learning layering of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 8 Tosada Shells
  • 3 Medium Tomatoes
  • 4 Medium Avocadoes
  • 3 tablespoons Red Onion
  • 0.25 cup Cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Chili Powder
  • 0.75 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 lime Juice
  • 15 onces Low Sodium Black Beans
  • 3 cups Chicken
  • 0.5 cup Cotija Cheese
  • Cooking Spray

Steps:

  1. Bake tosada shells: Line a large baking sheet with a silicone mat and place tortillas in a single layer. Spray both sides of the tortillas with cooking spray and a sprinkle of salt. Bake at 400 degrees F for 6 minutes per side.
  2. Make guacamole salsa: Mix tomato, avocado, red onion, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, salt and lime juice.
  3. Assemble tosadas: Top each shell with guacamole salsa, beans, chicken and cheeese
